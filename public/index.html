Delicious Cinnamon Buns

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk (110°F)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs

Filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, butter, salt, and eggs. Add the yeast mixture and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a large rectangle.
  6. Spread melted butter over the dough and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Roll up the dough tightly, starting from the long side.
  8. Cut the rolled dough into 12 even pieces and place them in a greased baking pan.
  9. Cover and let the buns rise for another 30 minutes.
  10. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the buns for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  11. While the buns are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting by mixing cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  12. Spread the frosting over the cooled cinnamon buns and enjoy!
