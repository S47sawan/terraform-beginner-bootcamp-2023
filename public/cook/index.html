Cinnamon Buns Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk. Add sugar and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix flour, melted butter, salt, and eggs into the yeast mixture.
  3. Knead the dough until it's smooth and elastic.
  4. Roll the dough into a rectangle.
  5. Spread softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon on the dough.
  6. Roll up the dough and cut into buns.
  7. Place buns in a greased baking pan and let them rise.
  8. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.
